About Us

Founder’s Vision

The founding vision of ICST is rooted in the belief that higher education should be a transformative force capable of breaking down socio-economic barriers. ICST was established with the explicit purpose of providing a job oriented higher education for those with limited financial means, creating an environment where every aspiring student, regardless of their economic background, can access quality education and embark on a journey of enfranchisement.

The founder envisioned a university with inclusivity that ensure students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds have equal opportunities to pursue higher education. The founder’s vision is a commitment to social responsibility too. ICST engages in initiatives that address societal challenges, contributing to the betterment of communities and emphasizing the role of higher education in creating positive social change, and contributing towards human resources development of our country.
